What is Arsolar?

Arsolar contains the current factual information about the contents of the solar system and beyond. It aims to enlighten the misconceptions about the heavenly bodies and shed light to the astronomical processes. The website shows visual representations and videos, so the viewer can get a grasp of these subjects in astronomical proportions. It will also include other information outside of the solar system.
